# AI Finance Analyst

A Python-based multi-agent system for analysing small business finances.
Built for New Zealand professional service SMEs (1-10 employees).

## What It Does

Takes raw financial data (from Xero, MYOB, Excel exports) and produces:
- **Normalised data** - Clean, structured financial information
- **Performance analysis** - Revenue, margins, expenses, cash flow insights
- **Risk assessment** - Early warning signs rated by severity
- **Scenario analysis** - "What if" projections (revenue drop, cost increase, etc.)
- **Recommendations** - 3-5 prioritised, actionable items
- **Plain-English report** - Ready to share with the business owner
